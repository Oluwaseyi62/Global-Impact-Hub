About Founder

Oluwaferanmi Famawode is a dynamic young leader who is passionate about empowering teenagers and young adults to discover their purpose, live a purposeful life, and make a positive impact on the world

I have spoken to over 2500 teenagers and young adults on topics such as purpose discovery, purpose living, educational benefits, and many more. I am also an ardent lover of books, writing, creative, and humanity.

In addition to my work as a speaker, I have also organized free health seminars for the old and young, educational seminars, and research works. I have also contributed to my society by partnering with various companies and organizations to bring something worthwhile to the youthful age.

Oluwaferanmi is a role model for teenagers and young adults everywhere. I am a shining example of what can be achieved when you are passionate about making a difference in the world.

Here are some of Oluwaferanmi's accomplishments:

* Spoken to over 2500 teenagers and young adults on purpose discovery, purpose living, educational benefits, and many more topics

* Organized free health seminars for the old and young

* Organized educational seminars

* Conducted research works

* Contributed to his society

* Has over 16 Certificates on Human Skills and Development

* Partnered with various companies and organizations to bring something worthwhile to the youthful age

Oluwaferanmi is an inspiration to all. I am a true leader who is making a difference in the world.
